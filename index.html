Connect

Find People and Groups in your community who have relevant interests and skills who you can join your political or civic cause or who can invite you to join theirs. There are several platforms that can help you find like-minded people but ChangeMkr is designed specifically for you to “match” and collaborate with them.

Engage

Find Events, Actions and Content from your community or anywhere where you can participate and have your voice heard. There are several platforms that can help you find relevant opportunities for political and civic involvement but ChangeMkr cuts through the noise to aggregate them in an easily-searchable directory.

Empower

Create and Share ways for anyone of any experience level to get involved online or offline in the campaigns and causes we care about. Most political and civic communities rely on stove-piped calls to action that come from the “top down” but ChangeMkr is truly a "bottom up" collaborative grassroots tool for supporter-led 21st Century organizing via an Open CRM platform rarely seen since My.BarackObama.com debuted in 2007.
